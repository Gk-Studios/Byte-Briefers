What do we do?

We are Byte Briefers and we like to post up summarys about new scientific topics or discoveries most people don't know about or how they work. It started out when we saw a article talking about a new scientific discovery that he was fascinated by. So we thought why not turn this into a website. And sometime and work later, we created Byte Briefers.

* We always liked news but we would always encounter paywalls like "pay for subscription to continue reading" and we got tired of always having to pay for news. So we decided to create a small alternative which we call Byte Briefers.
